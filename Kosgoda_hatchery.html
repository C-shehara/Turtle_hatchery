ABOUT US

Welcome to Kosgoda Hatchery, a renowned turtle conservation center in Sri Lanka. Our hatchery is dedicated to protecting and preserving the endangered sea turtles that nest along the beautiful shores of Kosgoda. At our facility, we work tirelessly to ensure the survival of these magnificent creatures through various conservation efforts. Join us on a journey to learn about sea turtles, witness the hatching of adorable baby turtles, and contribute to their conservation. Together, let's make a difference and secure a future for these incredible marine species.
